This card has a primary colored border at the top, indicating its importance or primary status.

Success Card

A success-styled card typically used for positive confirmations or successful operations.

Warning Card

Warning cards draw attention to important information that requires the user's awareness.

Danger Card

Danger cards are used for critical alerts, errors, or destructive actions that need caution.

Cards with Tools

Collapsible Card

Click the minus button in the header to collapse this card. Click it again to expand.

This is useful for long content that users might want to hide temporarily.

Maximizable Card

Click the expand button to maximize this card to fullscreen mode.

Perfect for detailed content or data tables that need more space.

Removable Card

Click the X button to remove this card from the page.

Useful for dismissible notifications or temporary content.

All Tools

This card has all three tools: collapse, maximize, and remove.

Gives users full control over their dashboard layout.
